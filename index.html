Hot Wheels

As part of the second place team at a 24 hour hackathon at RamHacks 2019 I acted as a full-stack developer and Unity designer to create Hot Wheels.     

Hot Wheels is a solution made for CarMax that uses alternate reality to virtually show which cars have been reserved and which cars are available for sale.    

See RamHacks 2019 Webpage

VCU Police Web Master

I am one of two web masters for the Virginia Commonwealth University Police Department. I use Terminal Four CMS to keep the website up to date.      

A major goal of my work on the VCU Police website has been to ensure accessibility for people who use screen readers or have difficulties using a standard keyboard and mouse. 

See Police Website

Boids!

Boids is an artificial life program, developed by Craig Reynolds in 1986, which simulates the flocking behaviour of birds. This particular experiment uses P5.js to simulate flocking.

This project was inspired by Daniel Shiffman from The Coding Train.

Go ahead and play around with the sliders now!
